## Overview

Built an XGBoost model using Python with USU football and broader collegiate data to predict which players are most likely to enter the transfer portal. Earned 3rd place overall in the Sports Analytics competition.

---

## Problem

Transfer portal decisions significantly impact team composition and recruiting strategy. Identifying players at risk of transferring before they enter the portal allows coaching staff to proactively address retention or adjust recruiting pipelines.

---

## Solution

- Collected USU football and broader collegiate player performance data
- Engineered features relevant to transfer portal decisions
- Trained and tuned an XGBoost classification model to predict portal likelihood
- Visualized player gain and loss patterns by college in Tableau
