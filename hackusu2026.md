## Overview

Designed and executed a conjoint analysis survey at the market research level to evaluate a new product line for Red Button Vintage Creamery. Identified a potential profit of over $3 per unit within the Logan college student segment and earned 1st place for the final project presentation.

---

## Problem

Red Button Vintage Creamery needed to understand whether a new product line would resonate with their core Logan market and at what price point it would generate meaningful profit.

---

## Solution

- Designed a conjoint analysis survey targeting Logan college students
- Analyzed attribute-level utilities to identify the highest-value product configuration
- Identified a viable price point generating $3+ profit per unit
- Presented findings and business recommendations to judges
